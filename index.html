Welcome

I am a full-time research scholar in the Department of Electrical Engineering at the University of Southern California, affiliated with the Wireless Devices and Systems (WiDeS) Group, under the supervision of Professor Andreas F. Molisch. Prior to that, I received my M.S. and Ph.D. degrees from the Department of Electronic Engineering, UESTC and SJTU in 2015 and 2019, respectively. My advisor was Professor Robert C. Qiu.

As an interdisciplinary researcher, my research interests include integrating data physics into neural network optimization and the related Wireless Communications and Smart Sensing applications (In the computer science domain, it is related to the tasks of time series generation, prediction, and anomaly detection). I am dedicated to reproducible research and have made many codes publicly available. Please visit detailed projects and related codes.
